About Us

Learn more about our family business and commitment to quality

Glendee Fertilizer and Lime is a family-owned business based in Dundee, KZN. With over four decades of experience in the agricultural industry, we have established ourselves as trusted partners to farmers throughout Northern KwaZulu-Natal.


We proudly represent top brands like Kynoch, Pistorious, Kalkor, SA Kalk en Gips, Link Seed, and Wenkem, servicing areas from Ladysmith to Paulpietersburg. Our commitment to quality and service has made us the preferred agricultural supplier in the region.
